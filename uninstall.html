Uninstall Instructions

Follow the directions below to uninstall PDFStunner

1. On the desktop, Right click on PDFStunner's shortcut and Click on Delete or
2. Press Win key + E to open file explorer

3. After opening file explorer, In address bar type "%AppData%\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu" and press enter
4. Right-click on the "PDFStunner" folder and select delete or

Restore search settings in Microsoft Edge

Step 1: Perform a search in the address bar using the search engine you want to set as your default.
Step 2: Select Settings and more... > Settings.

Step 3: Select Privacy, search, and services

Step 4: Scroll all the way down to the Services Services section and select Address bar and search.

Step 5: Choose your preferred search engine from the Search engine used in the address bar menu.

Restore search settings in Microsoft Edge

Step 1: Go to Google Chrome main menu at the top right corner (three dots)
Step 2: Click "Settings"
Step 3: On the left menu items Click on "Search engine"
Step 4: Toggle through the search engines and choose the desired search engine
