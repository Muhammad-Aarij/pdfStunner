Uninstall Instructions

How to Uninstall

Windows

1. Open the **Start Menu** and select **Settings**.
2. Go to **Apps** and then click on **Apps & Features**.
3. Find the program in the list of installed apps.
4. Click on it and select **Uninstall**.
5. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the uninstallation process.

macOS

1. Open the **Finder**.
2. Go to the **Applications** folder.
3. Find the app you want to uninstall.
4. Right-click on the app and select **Move to Trash**.
5. Empty the Trash to complete the process.

Android

1. Open the **Settings** app.
2. Go to **Apps** or **Applications**.
3. Find the app in the list and select it.
4. Tap **Uninstall**.
5. Confirm the uninstallation to remove the app.

iOS

1. Locate the app on your **Home Screen**.
2. Press and hold the app icon until a menu appears.
3. Tap **Remove App** or **Delete App**.
4. Confirm to remove the app from your device.

Browser Extensions

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.).
2. Go to the **Extensions** or **Add-ons** menu.
3. Find the extension in the list of installed extensions.
4. Click the **Remove** or **Delete** button.
5. Confirm the uninstallation process to remove the extension.
